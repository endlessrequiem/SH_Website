The Challenge:

Solve a problem in your local city, county or the State of California, in 12 hours or less. Your Solution could be anything from a mobile application, to a website, to server-side code. As long as it gets the job done.

Who can participate?

Everyone of all skill levels are welcome to participate. Problem solving could be anything from simple code to a complex applications. Potential is key here.
